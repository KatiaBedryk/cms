# Frequently Asked Questions

## How do I get started with TechVision?

Reach out through our contact page or email us directly. We'll schedule a free 30-minute discovery call to understand your needs and see if we're a good fit.

## What industries do you work with?

We work across many industries including finance, healthcare, retail, education, and manufacturing. Our solutions are tailored to each industry's specific requirements and regulations.

## How long does a typical project take?

It depends on the scope:
- **Small projects** (landing pages, simple tools): 2-4 weeks
- **Medium projects** (web applications, APIs): 2-3 months
- **Large projects** (enterprise platforms, migrations): 6-12 months

We always start with a detailed estimate before beginning any work.

## Do you offer support after launch?

Yes! We offer ongoing maintenance and support packages. We also provide training for your team so they can handle basic updates independently.

## What is your pricing model?

We offer both fixed-price and time-and-materials pricing:
- **Fixed price** — best for well-defined projects with clear requirements
- **Time & materials** — best for evolving projects or ongoing engagements

## Can you work with our existing tech stack?

Absolutely. We're technology-agnostic and can work with your current infrastructure. We'll recommend changes only when they provide clear business value.
