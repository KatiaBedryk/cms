# Getting Started

Welcome! This guide will help you understand how to work with TechVision and what to expect from our engagement process.

## Step 1: Discovery Call

We start with a free 30-minute call to understand:
- Your business goals and challenges
- Current technology landscape
- Timeline and budget expectations

## Step 2: Proposal

Based on our conversation, we'll prepare a detailed proposal including:
- Recommended approach and technology stack
- Project timeline with milestones
- Cost estimate and payment schedule
- Team composition

## Step 3: Kickoff

Once the proposal is approved:
1. We assign a dedicated project manager
2. We schedule a kickoff meeting with all stakeholders
3. We set up communication channels (Slack, Jira, or your preferred tools)
4. Development begins!

## Step 4: Development & Delivery

During development:
- **Weekly demos** — see progress every week
- **Bi-weekly reports** — track timeline and budget
- **Continuous feedback** — we iterate based on your input

## Step 5: Launch & Support

After launch:
- We monitor the application for the first 2 weeks
- We fix any issues that arise at no extra cost
- We hand over documentation and training materials
- Ongoing support is available if needed
