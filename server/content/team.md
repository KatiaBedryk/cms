## Culture

We value:
- **Continuous learning** — weekly tech talks and conference attendance
- **Work-life balance** — flexible hours and remote work options
- **Open communication** — flat hierarchy, open-door policy
- **Quality over speed** — we'd rather do it right than do it fast
